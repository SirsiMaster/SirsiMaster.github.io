Market Overview

The cloud infrastructure optimization market is undergoing a paradigm shift from traditional Infrastructure as Code (IaC) solutions to AI-driven autonomous infrastructure management. This transition is driven by increasing complexity in cloud operations, rising costs, and the need for predictive optimization.

Key market drivers include:

  • Rising cloud infrastructure costs (projected 22% increase in 2025)
  • Increasing complexity of multi-cloud environments
  • Growing demand for autonomous operations
  • Shift towards predictive optimization

Market Size & Growth Trajectory

The cloud infrastructure optimization market is projected to grow from $78.8B in 2025 to $276.5B by 2030, representing a CAGR of 28.5%. This growth is primarily driven by:

  • Enterprise digital transformation initiatives
  • Adoption of AI-driven infrastructure management
  • Increasing focus on cost optimization
  • Growing complexity of cloud architectures
