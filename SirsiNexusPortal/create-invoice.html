PayPal Native Invoicing (Recommended)

For more professional invoicing, use PayPal's built-in system:

  1. Go to paypal.com/invoice
  2. Click "Create Invoice"
  3. Fill in your client details and services
  4. PayPal will send professional branded invoices
  5. Clients can pay directly through PayPal's secure system
  6. You'll get automatic payment notifications

Benefits: Professional branding, automatic reminders, tax reporting, and built-in dispute resolution.
